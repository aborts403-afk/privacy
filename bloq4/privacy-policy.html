Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Bloq4 - Brain Puzzle ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Bloq4 is designed with your privacy in mind. We minimize data collection:

  • Game Progress: Your puzzle completion status, star ratings, and hint usage are stored locally on your device only using SharedPreferences. This data is never transmitted to any server.
  • Purchase Information: If you make an in-app purchase (e.g., ad removal), the transaction is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store payment information.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob

  • We display banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded video ads through Google AdMob.
  • AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Play Billing

  • In-app purchases are processed through Google Play's billing system.
  • See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

3. Advertising ID & Tracking

  • On iOS, we request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) consent before accessing the advertising identifier.
  • On Android, the advertising ID may be used by AdMob for ad personalization.
  • You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

4. Children's Privacy

Bloq4 is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The ads served are configured to comply with applicable regulations.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All game data is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not operate servers or databases that store user data.
  • Uninstalling the App will delete all locally stored game progress.

6. Your Rights

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can:

  • Clear app data through your device settings to reset all progress.
  • Uninstall the App to remove all associated data.
  • Opt out of personalized ads through your device's ad settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
